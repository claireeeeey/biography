Claude Monet

French Painter

Claude Monet was a famous French painter and was seen as the founder of impressionist painting. Some of his notbale work includes Impression, Sunrise, Water Lilies and the Japanese Bridge, and Woman with a Parasol.

He was good at the painting method of light and shadow. Although there was no clear shadow outline in Monet's painting, his delicate use of color depicted obects in an unique way in different light.
